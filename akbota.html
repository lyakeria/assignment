APRIL 20| 2005

My name literally means "little white camel". I really like it, because my grandmother gave it to me at birth.


I was born and lived in Kokshetau, until one day I moved to the capital. Now, Astana is the most comfortable city for me, where I would like to live further.


For many years I played the piano, which was my main hobby. Creativity and art criticism have always been closer to me, since the age of four I have been engaged in folk-style dancing.


Now I am at the stage of searching for my favorite business, this is helped by my admission to the desired specialty.
